About Me

I am a PhD researcher in artificial intelligence in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Antwerp, focusing on developing advanced deep-learning methods for hyperspectral image analysis. My research focuses on enhancing the representation and interpretation of hyperspectral data through cutting-edge machine-learning approaches.
